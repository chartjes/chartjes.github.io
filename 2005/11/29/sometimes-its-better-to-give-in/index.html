Sometimes It's Better To Give In post

Recently I switched my other blog over to using the Typo blog engine instead of the one I had written myself. It's written in Rails as well, so I will be able to go into it and hack around. First task is to add some captcha stuff to the comments.

I don't get tons of traffic to that blog, but I hate comment spammers with a passion. With captcha in there, it will get rid of all but the most hardcore of spammers. Which is good enough for me.
