Freckles Are Cute!
post

Congratulations to my meet-once-but-talk-all-the-time-via-IM friend Amy Hoy for winning the RailsDay Contest for the app she wrote with her frind Bryan Wood called Freckle, an online app for keeping track of wedding stuff.

I know that Amy is getting married "some time soon" (but not to Bryan) so it's no surprise that she came up with something like this. There's a great breakdown of the winners over at Evan Weaver's blog

Congrats Amy!
